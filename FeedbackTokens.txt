### These are feedback tokens which the Speech Recognition Engine ###
### will dynamically expect, after completing an 'Evaluate' task, ###
### i.e. responding to a question such as "What color is this?"   ###

### DO NOT MODIFY THE FIRST TWO COLUMNS ###

2 : Yes	  : Yes, Right, Correct
3 : Good  : Good, Good Job, Nice
4 : Great : Great, Great Job, Fantastic, Excellent
5 : MehNo : Um
6 : No    : No, Wrong, Sorry
7 : Bad   : Bad
8 : Awful : Awful, Terrible, Horrible, You Suck
